Aksar poochhe jaane waale sawaal

Kya sabhi stores par ek hi offer milta hai?

Zyada offers sabhi stores par same hote hain, lekin kuch prices store ke hisaab se thoda alag ho sakte hain. Website par apna store select karein taaki aapko sahi rate dikhe.

Store timing kya hai?

Zyadatar Rajmandir stores subah 9 baje se raat 9 baje tak khule rehte hain. Apne nazdeeki store ka exact time "Stores" page par dekhein.

Kya aap home delivery karte hain?

Abhi hum walk-in wholesale-rate shopping par focus karte hain. Naye offers ke liye WhatsApp par judein — har hafte ka pamphlet aapko mil jaayega.

Offers kitni baar update hote hain?

Offers roz update hote hain aur har hafte naya pamphlet aata hai. Homepage par "Updated today" tag se aapko pata chal jaata hai.
